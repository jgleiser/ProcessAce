Order Fulfillment Process

1. The process begins when the Customer places an order on the website.
2. The System validates the payment details provided by the Customer.
3. If the payment is invalid:
    a. The System sends a "Payment Failed" notification to the Customer.
    b. The process ends.
4. If the payment is valid:
    a. The System sends a "Payment Successful" notification.
    b. The Warehouse receives the order details and packs the items.
    c. The Carrier picks up the package and ships it to the Customer address.
    d. The Customer receives the package.
5. The process ends.
