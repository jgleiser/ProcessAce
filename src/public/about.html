About ProcessAce

ProcessAce helps teams turn interviews, recordings, and documents into structured process documentation.

What ProcessAce Does

ProcessAce is an AI-powered process discovery and documentation application built for self-hosted teams.

It transforms source material into BPMN diagrams, SIPOC tables, RACI matrices, and narrative process documentation.
